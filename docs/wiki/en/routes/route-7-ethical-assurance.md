# Route 7: Ethical Assurance

> Source: `sections/routes/route-7-ethical-assurance.md`

## Summary

Triggered by questions about hidden bias, ethical assumptions, or value conflicts across teams at different scales ("how do I audit bias in our process?", "values clash between engineers and ops — what's the map?"). Output is a bias register, a conflict map grouped by scale (individual / team / org / ecosystem), and an ethical-audit checklist.

## Key Decisions

- **Chain length:** 5 sections in full load, 3 core.
- **Core sections:** `D.2` (multi-scale ethics framework), `D.3` (holonic conflict topology), `D.5` (bias audit & ethical assurance).
- **Full chain:** adds `D.1` (axiological neutrality) and `D.4` (trust-aware mediation calculus).

## Status

Active. Used for the `ethical_assurance` burden. Reasoner template: "Conflict map by scale → Bias register (type / location / risk / mitigation) → Audit checklist".
